About Us

Welcome to our organization! We strive to make the world a better place by reducing food waste and helping those in need. Our mission is to unite partners and volunteers to save food products and ensure their fair distribution.

We collaborate with cafes, restaurants, shops, and other food suppliers to collect surplus food and direct it to where it is truly needed. Our team works with love and care so that everyone can contribute to the common cause.

♻️ One of the key goals of our project is environmental care. Food waste releases methane—a greenhouse gas dozens of times more harmful than carbon dioxide. It pollutes soil and water, increases the carbon footprint, and contributes to global warming. By reducing food waste, we not only help people but also protect nature from irreversible harm.

Join us and become part of the solution—together we can change the future!
